destination wedding venues in rajasthan

Romance, drama, history, and beauty – everything comes together in regal Rajasthan. If the thought of being a princess bride or a majestic groom at your fairytale wedding excites you, nothing beats Rajasthan when it comes to locations and venues. The desert state’s ancient forts, marble palaces, lush gardens, cinematic pavilions, serene lakes, and sand dunes offer unique settings and unparalleled backdrops for any celebration. Devi Ratn, Jaipur – IHCL SeleQtions

This place offering contemporary luxury with traditional charm promises authentic experiences and unforgettable stays to the wedding squad

fort khejarla,jodhpur

Around 400 years of history have made Jodhpur’s Fort Khejarla an impressive showcase of Rajput valor and lifestyle. Managed by the royal family of Kherjala, this grand property with red sandstone pillars, stone carved rooms, windows with latticework, intricate jharokhas, and imposing exteriors is perfect for a high-powered intimate wedding.

udai-kothi-udaipur

rambagh-palace,jaipur

This regal architectural marvel truly epitomizes royal hospitality. Managed by the Taj Group, Rambagh Palace offers regality and modernity at the same time, and is a highly sought after property for couples looking for a royal wedding experience.”

Welcomhotel Khimsar Fort and Dunes

“With a year-round outdoor swimming pool, terrace, free parking, and on-site restaurant, this property provides both comfort and convenience to guests and visitors. Every room offers stunning views of the desert.”

fairmont,jaipur

The hotel exudes royal elegance and grandeur with its well-appointed rooms, exquisite architecture, and regal outdoor spaces.”

the ummed,jodhpur

This property is a kaleidoscope of gracious facets – an exclusive porch, pathways for baraat processions, long private driveways, open spaces, and indoor venues.

Shiv Vilas Resort, Jaipur

The royal ambiance with intricate architectural details and expert craftsmanship at this property offers a visual delight to all the guests.

suryagarh,jaisalmer

Suryagarh Jaisalmer is a fabulous yellow sandstone property situated along the old Silk Road route. Its warm, inviting exteriors are surrounded by stately gardens and courtyards that offer ample venue options for a regal wedding.

ITC Rajputana Jaipur

ITC Rajputana has a classic and authentic appeal which attracts a lot of millennial couples wanting to host a destination wedding in Jaipur. It has adequate rooms to accommodate large wedding groups.”

The Westin Pushkar Resort & Spa

For an opulent wedding that is high on style and splendor, head to The Westin Pushkar Resort & Spa. Surrounded by the breathtaking views of the Aravalli mountains and golden dunes, this magnificent property is famous for its seemingly endless lawns, a world-class spa, and serene ambiance

Mundota Palace and Resort, Jaipur

Deemed as India’s first luxury heritage palace resort, the 15th-century Mundita Fort & Palace is a beautiful showcase of Rajput heritage with its courtyards, terraces, charming pavilions, and gardens. Just south of this palatial property is an active polo ground with around 80 stables housing some of the most thoroughbred horses in India.
