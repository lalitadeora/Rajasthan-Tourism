About Jaipur

Jaipur, also known as the pink city, is one of the most beautiful and culturally rich cities in India. There are many historical places to visits there, the government earns much royalty from these historical destinations. Hawa Mahal does not have any stairways and you can explore the insides by ramps. Jaipur is also known as 'Pink city' in the world. Some of the popular heritage points of tourist interest in Jaipur are Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, Sunehri kothi, Jantar Mantar, Jai Mahal, Rambagh palace, Rajmahal Palace and many others. The Nahargarh Fort is the best location to go if you want to see the entire city of Jaipur. The former Maharaja of Jaipur built this fort to enhance local security. The former name of Jai Singh II's Nahargarh Fort was Sudarshangarh. Jaipur organizes several fairs and celebrates various festivals including the Gangaur festival, Teej festival, Jaipur Literature festival, Chaksu Fair, Elephant Fair and Shitla Mata Fair.
