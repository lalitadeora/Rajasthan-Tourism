About sawai madhopur

Sawai Madhopur is famous for its guavas, which are popularly referred as 'Madhopur Guavas' in Rajasthan. The place is not only known for its wildlife, but also for its historical importance. The rich and glorious past of the place is reflected in the magnificent Ranthambore Fort and other historical monuments , This is one of the reasons why tiger tours are so popular in the area. With 40 species of mammals, 320 species of birds, 40 Species of Reptiles, 02 amphibian species, 50 species of Butterflies, and 300 species of plants, Ranthambore is perfect for any wildlife enthusiast.
